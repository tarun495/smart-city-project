Local Tips

🚌 Transport

Use city buses for short distances. Auto-rickshaws are metered — always insist on meter. Avoid peak hours (8-10 AM, 5-7 PM).

🍽️ Food

Try local thali for lunch — best value. Street food is excellent but stick to busy stalls. Avoid raw salads from unknown vendors.

💰 Money

ATMs available in all main markets. Bargaining is expected in local bazaars. Most hotels accept cards but keep cash handy.

📱 Connectivity

Local SIM cards available at the airport and central market. Most cafes and hotels offer free WiFi. Download offline maps before you go.

🕌 Culture
