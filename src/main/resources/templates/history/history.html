🏛️ City History

Discover the rich heritage of our city

🏺 Ancient History

The city dates back to ancient times, with evidence of early settlements along the river banks. Archaeological findings suggest habitation as early as the 3rd century BC. The region flourished as a trade hub connecting the northern and southern parts of the subcontinent.

⚔️ Medieval Period

During the medieval era, the city came under the rule of several dynasties. Each ruler left a distinct architectural and cultural imprint — from grand forts to intricate temples and mosques that still stand today as heritage monuments.

🇮🇳 Colonial Era & Independence

The British colonial period transformed the city into an administrative and commercial centre. The city played a crucial role in the independence movement, with several notable freedom fighters hailing from this region. Post-independence, rapid industrialisation brought significant demographic and economic changes.

🌆 Modern City

Today, the city is a thriving metropolis with a diverse economy spanning IT, manufacturing, trade and tourism. The city government continues to invest in smart infrastructure, public transport and green spaces to improve the quality of life for its residents.
